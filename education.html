Where it all began.

It all started for me in the local pre-school in Glounthaune. The Early Years Centre is a fine institute for early dvelopment and learning and set me on my path to where I am today. I'm not going to lie, I can't remember anything about my time there other than the generic crap I just gave you so lets just move on to my time in primary school.

The Story Continues.

Moving on to the next chapter of my education, I began studying... yes studying... In Glounthaune Primary School or to give it its proper name Scoil Náisiúnta an Chroí Naofa in 2005. I learned many valuable skills throughout my time in primary school from matching and colouring to counting. Some would argue that I hit my peak in fifth class when I was eight time champ of our running game of last man standing but I would beg to differ, for me, the best is yet to come.

The Story Continues... Again

It is safe to say that I entered secondary school in Christian Brothers' College a boy and left... Well not a man but a bigger boy or manchild if you will. I thuroughly enjoyed my time in C.B.C and made many friends that I hope to keep in contact with in the future. From the atmosphere on senior cup game day to the heated debates over the most pointless topics at lunch time, I wouldn't change a thing about my experience in second level education. Because this is where I did the majority of my information absorption, I will split this into two sections, Junior Cycle and Leaving Cycle.

The Dreaded Leaving Cert

The Leaving Cert is without a doubt one of the toughest obstacles I have had to overcome in life thus far. The pressure and stress students are under is incomparable to anything else I have experienced. The many hours studying information that on the most part would be completely irrelevant to me for the rest of my life was a tough grind. However, having come out the other side, I am immensely proud of my efforts and results in the exams which got me into the course I've always wanted to study, CK401 or Computer Science in UCC Below is a table of the subjects I took for my Leaving Cert along with their levels and results.
